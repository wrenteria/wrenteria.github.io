About Me

I am a Research Assistant and PhD student at the USC Sonny Astani Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering. As a Lynett Wave Research Group member, he specializes in coastal hydrodynamics, focusing on developing hybrid numerical models.

Currently, I amleading the development of hybrid models that combine numerical and artificial intelligence components to solve complex coastal problems.

Originally from Ecuador, I hold a Master’s degree in Oceanography from Texas A&M University. Prior to joining USC, I gained extensive experience at the Oceanographic Institute of the Navy in Ecuador where I served as both the head of the Oceanography department and the chief of the tsunami warning center.
